Everyone leads different lives; friendships change and we all go our separate ways. We realise this as we mature and grow into adults.

This film is about three girls and two boys who were all best friends in primary school but sadly lost contact with eachother. Nine years later, fate brings them back together for a school reunion. Will the friendship be the same? Or is their reunion destined to cause drama and chaos?
